    • Welcome

    Church who supports the community spiritually and practically. We are a small group of believer’s who meet at Parker’s Road We are an Acapella Church and we welcome you to our Sunday Service – 10am

    Music

    We are an acapella congregation, meaning we worship through unaccompanied singing. Our music is led by members of our congregation.

    Service

    Our Sunday service begins at 10am and includes prayer, acapella singing, Bible reading, and a sermon. All are welcome to join us.

    Other

    We are involved in supporting our local community and missions work. We welcome visitors and new members.
